From the owner
“I don't want you to need me every four weeks. I want your hair to behave on a Tuesday when you slept wrong and it's raining. That's the job.”

June Calloway · Owner, Verbena

June opened Verbena in 2021 after nine years behind other people's chairs. The studio keeps four stylists, one coffee pot, and a strict no-upsell policy — if your hair doesn't need it, nobody here will sell it to you.
